Japan Tour 2026

About the Tour

In 2026 the BOH Hamburg Orchestra will travel to Japan for a special concert tour. The tour will create a musical and cultural bridge between Hamburg and Japan.

Young musicians will perform concerts, meet orchestras in Japan and experience one of the most exciting music cultures in the world.

Support the Tour

To make this tour possible we are raising funds for travel, instruments transport and concert organisation.
