Japan Tour 2026

About the Tour

In 2026 the BOH Hamburg Orchestra will travel to Japan for a special concert tour. The tour will build a cultural bridge between Hamburg and Japan through music and collaboration.

The orchestra will perform concerts, meet Japanese musicians and represent Hamburg in one of the most exciting music cultures in the world.
