Japan Tour 2026

About the Tour

In 2026 the BOH Hamburg Orchestra will travel to Japan for a special concert tour. The tour will create a cultural bridge between Hamburg and Japan through music, collaboration and live performances.

The orchestra will perform concerts, meet Japanese musicians and represent Hamburg in one of the world's most exciting music cultures.

Support the Tour

To make this tour possible we are raising funds for travel, instrument transport and concert organisation.
